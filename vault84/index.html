Features

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

No bloat, no dark patterns, no upsells. Just a finance app that works.

🔒

Completely offline

Vault84 makes zero network requests. Your data never leaves your phone. There's no internet permission in the app manifest.

💰

Budget tracking

Track income, bills, savings goals, and spending. See exactly where your money goes each month without over-complicating it.

🔔

Bill reminders

Optional notifications so bills never catch you off guard. Set it up once, forget about it — until you need it.

👆

Biometric lock

Lock the app with your phone's existing fingerprint, face ID, or PIN. Optional, and entirely under your control.

💾

Backup and restore

Export your data to anywhere you choose — Google Drive, local storage, wherever. We never see your backup files.

🚫

Zero ads, ever

No advertising networks, no analytics SDKs, no third-party tracking of any kind. Vault84 is built to serve you, not sell you.

Privacy

Your data. Your phone. Full stop.

Here's exactly what Vault84 does and doesn't do with your information.

Notifications

Used only for optional bill reminders. Can be disabled at any time in Settings.

Biometric authentication

Uses your phone's existing Face ID, fingerprint, or PIN to lock the app. Entirely optional.

Boot completed

So your scheduled bill reminders survive a phone restart. Nothing else.

Camera, microphone, location

Never requested. Explicitly blocked. Vault84 has no use for any of these.

Contacts, calendar, storage

Never requested. Explicitly blocked. Your personal data is none of our business.

Internet access

Vault84 is 100% offline. It makes zero network requests. There is no server. Your financial data never goes anywhere.
